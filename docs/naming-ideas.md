# App Naming Ideas

Here are some creative and modern name ideas for the video downloader app, categorized by the vibe you might want to go for:

## Minimalist & Modern (Clean & Professional)
*   **Vidl** (Short for Video Downloader, simple and memorable)
*   **Fetchr** (Focuses on the action of fetching content)
*   **AeroGrab** (Implies speed and lightness)
*   **Snag** (Direct, action-oriented)
*   **Orbit Downloader** (Sounds comprehensive and encompassing)
*   **Luma Video** (Gives it a polished, premium software feel)

## Descriptive & Direct (Clear about what it does)
*   **AnyDL** (Implies it can download from *any* of the 1000+ supported sites)
*   **MediaCatch** (Broad enough to cover video and audio)
*   **yt-gui** (Very literal, good if you want to explicitly tie it to `yt-dlp`)
*   **StreamSave** (Clear and professional)
*   **LinkDrop** (Focuses on the user action of dropping a link)

## Creative & Catchy (More brandable)
*   **Yonder** (Evokes the idea of bringing things from "out there" to your local machine)
*   **Knot** (Tying the web to your local drive)
*   **Magneto** (Like a magnet pulling video files to your computer)
*   **Tether** (Connecting you to your media)
*   **Vault** (Emphasizes keeping videos safely stored locally)

## Top Recommendations
1.  **AnyDL:** It perfectly communicates the power of `yt-dlp` (that it works on almost *any* site, not just YouTube).
2.  **Fetchr:** It fits the very modern, clean UI we just built. It sounds like a sleek Mac app.
3.  **Vidl:** It's short, punchy, and easy to turn into a cool logo.
